# HandyYi Privacy Policy

<p class="updated">Last updated: May 31, 2026</p>

This privacy policy applies to the **HandyYi** Android app, published by **Ridgeline Labs** ("we", "us", "our").

## Summary

**HandyYi does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, on your device or anywhere else.** The app contains no networking code, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no advertising. It cannot phone home because it has no ability to phone anywhere.

## Information We Collect

None. HandyYi does not collect any information about you or your device.

## Local Storage

HandyYi stores the following on your device only, using standard Android preferences:

- **App preferences** — your chosen theme (light / dark / system), text size, yarrow-stalk animation toggle, and content language (Simplified Chinese / Traditional Chinese / English).

That is the complete list. No divination results, no usage history, no identifiers. Uninstalling the app removes all of it.

## Permissions

HandyYi requests no permissions beyond the Android defaults granted to every app. It does not access location, contacts, storage, camera, microphone, or any sensor.

## Network Access

HandyYi makes no network connections. The full hexagram text and all translations are bundled with the app.

## Third-Party Services and SDKs

HandyYi uses no third-party services or SDKs. There is no Google Analytics, no Firebase, no advertising network, no crash reporter.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not have any data to share.

## Children's Privacy

HandyYi is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no information at all, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children either.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
